Minecraft??

Minecraft is a sandbox-style video game created by Markus "Notch" Persson and later developed by Mojang Studios.
It rose to popularity due to its unique creative freedom and survival mechanics, allowing players to build and explore a blocky 3D world.

Since its release in 2011, Minecraft has become a cultural phenomenon, with millions of players worldwide and a vast community of content creators.
Despite being released over a decade ago, Minecraft continues to evolve and thrive, with regular updates and new features being added.
Its enduring popularity is a testament to the game's timeless appeal and the creativity of its players.
